About Us

The Harvesters is a clan in Clash of Clans. The clan was founded due to a difficulty in finding quality clans. Searching clan after clan many had fatal flaws: inactive players, incompetent leadership, volgar language in the chat, etc. This clan was created by Nary and Sharon on February 1st, 2015 in a hopes to find a balance that will allow the clan to thrive and maintain a fun atmosphere.

Recruiting members in a clan that only has two members is quite difficult. Very few people want to join a clan that may not succeed and can't start a war. When a person does join they have a weak base and won't stick around for long. After many hours of recruiting and a large quantity of troop donations we had our first war.
