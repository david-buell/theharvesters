About Us

The Harvesters is a clan in Clash of Clans. The clan was founded due to a difficulty in finding quality clans. Most clans that are accepting new members have fatal flaws: inactive players, incompetent leadership, volgar language in the chat, etc. This clan was created by Nary and Sharon on February 1st, 2015 in a hopes to find a balance that will allow the clan to thrive while maintaining a fun atmosphere.

This clan is still very young. We are accepting anyone interested in staying active by participating in wars and donating troops. Wars are started a few hours after the previous war finishes with the exception of the occasional 24 hour break. This allows the clan leaders to collect the statistics from the war, hand out promotions/demotions, and recruit new members.
