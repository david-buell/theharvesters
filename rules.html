Rules

A society without rules results in chaos. These rules are in place for the protection of the clan. All members should read and abide by the rules if they want to stay in the clan. The rules are subject to change in order to stay relevant as the clan evolves.

General

  • Be polite and respectful in chat.
  • All members are required to use english when participating in the chat.
  • Do not frequently clan hop without the permission of the leader or a co-leader.
  • Participate in all wars.
  • Donate troops.
  • Have fun!

Elder

The rank of elder must be earned by achieving six stars on the battle field. It can also be taken away if the clasher doesn't abide by these rules.

Responsiblities
  • Assist in war strategy coordination.
  • Help lower level clan members with their attacks and base layout.
  • Warn and kick out members that are in violation of these rules. Let a co-leader or leader know if the clasher needs to be banned.
  • Invite and accept/reject join requests.

Co-leader

This rank cannot be earned at this time. Co-leaders are responible for helping with recruiting efforts, maintaining order in chat, and helping coordinate war attacks.

Demotions

A demotion will be given if a member is in violation of any of the clan's rules. This will happen after one or more warnings by an elder or leader and the situation doesn't get resolved.

Reasons for demotion
  • Frequent use of profanity
  • Not attacking in two consecutive wars.
  • Kicking out a member without a valid reason.
