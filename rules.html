Rules

A society without rules results in chaos. These rules are in place for the protection of the clan. All members should read and abide by the rules. Infractions will result in demotions or being kicked out of the clan. Contact a clan leader if you are unable to meet one or more of these rules.

General

  • Be polite and respectful in chat.
  • Do not frequently clan hop.
  • Donate troops.
  • Have fun!

Wars

  • Your war status may be inactive for up to 1 week.
  • You must earn at least one star in every two consecutive wars you participate in.
  • You must attack in your first war.
  • Your army camp and spell factory must be full before attacking in war.

Elder

The rank of elder must be earned by achieving six stars on the battle field. It can also be taken away if the clasher doesn't abide by these rules.

Responsiblities
  • Assist in war strategy coordination.
  • Help lower level clan members with their attacks and base layout.
  • Warn and kick out members that are in violation of these rules. Let a co-leader or leader know if the clasher needs to be banned.
  • Invite and accept/reject join requests.

Co-leader

There is one permanent co-leader at this time, Sharon. We have one other position that can be filled by someone in the clan. This member is elected and may lose the status without warning or a clear reason. A different elder in the clan can also be voted into the position at any time.

Requirements to be eligible
  • Maintain elder for two weeks in this clan
  • 1,000 troop donations per season (two weeks)
  • Active participation in the clan chat
  • Achieve top 10 star count in the clan
  • Achieve top 10 base rank in war
  • Apply for the position by informing the leader or a co-leader
  • Achieve the majority vote of the leader and co-leaders to be elected
Responsibilities
  • Assist with occasional recruiting campaigns
  • Help keep order in the clan and answer any questions.
  • Run wars on a rotational bases
    • Announce when the war will begin
    • Start the war within 24 hours of the previous war
    • Determine troop donation and war strategy
    • Verify the clan is ready for war before the preparation day is complete
    • Inspire other members to attack
    • Hand out promotions, demotions, and kick people in accordance to the clan rules.

Demotions

A demotion will be given if a member is in violation of any of the clan's rules. This will happen after one or more warnings by an elder or leader if the situation doesn't get resolved. If the member did not achieve elder or co-leader they will be kicked out of the clan.

Common reasons for demotion
  • Frequent use of profanity in chat
  • Failing to earn a star in two consecutive wars.
